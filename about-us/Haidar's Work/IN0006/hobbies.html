Hobbies

Gym

I am quite an active person, I go to the gym every day as I feel like it is a great stress reliever. Also, it reminds me that I am not weak. When I was 15, I was very ill and very weak. I am now healthy and want to stay that way, so it is important that I take care of my body and how I look.

Here is a video of me from 3 months ago deadlifting 160KG. I'm not as big on deadlifting as I was before as there's a higher risk of injury and very low rewards from it. I do deadlift from time to time just to test my strength. Right now I can deadlift 220KG, however, it has been a while since I do not wanna risk a back injury.

Badminton

I love playing Badminton as it is a sport that is very underrated, I can get very competitive and badminton is the perfect sport for that. You don't have to be good at it in order to enjoy it that's why I love it. It forces you to be quick and to be light on your feet. I used to be captain of my secondary school's badminton team and I had a great time. I was very passionate about the sport so it led me to enjoy my role.

Cricket

Cricket is another one of those underated sports, you have to be light on your feet for and it's a good feeling when you're able to catch the ball and get someone out or when you hit a banger of a shot.

Working

This is a bit of a weird one as I enjoy working at my job. Currently, I am a Team Leader at a coffee shop at London City Airport. Since I am in the morning team, I am there from 4:30 in the noon. This means that I am able to earn money and still have the rest of the day to myself. It gives me time to go to the gym, see friends and relax with family. I feel like evening shifts take away from your life and your whole day feels wasted working. Work also helps you build discipline as there are many times I would like to call in sick but I don't due to the fact that I work to earn for myself. If I am not on top of it myself then who will be.

It is important for me to build my discipline now as if I do not have any then I am going to go through life thinking everything will come easily. Alhumdilah, I have worked hard for everything that I am today, everyone complements my work ethic, and the fact that I can do it means that anyone can.
